Ever considered the financial consequences that an accident could have on you and your family?
For as little as €6.55* per month, you can take steps to protect yourself and your family from the potential hardship an accident could bring. It's easy to register and you'll be covered immediately.

It's something that nobody likes to think about but everyday thousands of accidents occur in the Republic of Ireland - many of them during normal everyday life!

Here are some of the reasons to consider getting Personal Accident Insurance:

  • You're guaranteed to be accepted and you'll have cover immediately.
  • You'll receive financial help in the event of a serious injury occurring whilst driving, at home, at work, playing sport or abroad.
  • You'll receive 24 hour a day cover for accidents that occur anywhere in the world.
  • We pay the benefit of your policy directly to you.
  • 'Everyday' injuries such as fractures, burns and dislocations are also covered.
  • Your premiums don't increase with age (cover available for 18 to 74 year olds).
  • You can select cover of up to €600,000 on our platinum product.
